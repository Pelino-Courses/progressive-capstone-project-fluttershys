## Reflection 

Working on the SukaApp concept as a team was both challenging and deeply rewarding. *what I learned through teamwork taught me a great deal more than I expected.*

** The way as a team we worked together:**  
Our team's communication was built around honesty and respect. when we disagreed on ideas, we used a simple voting approach that ensured no voice was lost. The decision to name the app "SukaApp" came from Member B's suggestion after the field research .it was a moment that showed how listening to each other produces better outcomes than any single person's idea.

**My Contribution:**  
For Task 1, I Interviewed the first and last members at kimironko and Remera. For Task 2, I contributed specifically to Momo  payment integration and low data mode. For Task 3, I led the setup of GitHub repo and meeting scheduling.

**What I Learned:**  
The field research surprised me most. I expected to already know what the problem was, but actually observing real vendors and buyers at Kimironko showed me that the friction is more elegant than I assumed. *field research is essential; it forces you to listen before you design.*

**What I'd Do Differently:**  
I would start the wireframing earlier. We spent more time on problem definition than expected, which compressed our design time. Next phase, I will propose a parallel workstream so research and initial design can overlap.
